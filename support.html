About Green305

Green305 is a mobile application designed to help users discover and explore eco-friendly locations, parks, nature trails, and sustainable businesses throughout Miami-Dade County, Florida.

Key Features:

  • Interactive map of green spaces and eco-friendly locations
  • Location-based check-in functionality
  • Distance calculation and navigation assistance
  • Information about environmental impact and conservation efforts
  • Personal exploration tracking and achievements

Frequently Asked Questions

Why does the app need access to my location?

Green305 uses your location to display nearby green spaces, calculate distances, and verify check-ins when you visit a location. Your location data is processed locally on your device and is not stored on external servers.

How do I check in at a location?

When you are within approximately 100 meters of a listed green space, the check-in button will become active. Tap the button to record your visit. You may check in once per day at each location.

Why can't I check in even though I'm at the location?

GPS accuracy can vary depending on environmental factors such as buildings, tree coverage, or weather conditions. Try moving to a more open area and wait a few seconds for your location to update. Ensure that location services are enabled for Green305 in your device settings.

How do I get directions to a green space?

Tap on any location to open its detail view, then tap the "Directions" button. This will open Apple Maps with directions to the selected location.

Can I suggest a new location to be added?

Yes. Please email us at help.ghostshot@gmail.com with the location name, address, and a brief description of why it should be included in Green305.

How do I delete my data?

All data (visit history, achievements, preferences) is stored locally on your device. To delete it, uninstall the application or navigate to Settings > General > iPhone Storage > Green305 > Delete App.

Is Green305 free to use?

Yes. Green305 is free to download and use, with no advertisements or in-app purchases.

Troubleshooting

Location Not Showing

  • Open Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services
  • Ensure Location Services is enabled
  • Find Green305 and select "While Using the App"
  • Restart the application

Locations Not Loading

  • Verify your internet connection is active
  • Close and reopen the application
  • If the issue persists, try uninstalling and reinstalling the app

App Performance Issues

  • Close other applications running in the background
  • Restart your device
  • Ensure you have the latest version of iOS installed
