Privacy Policy

Green305 Application

1. Introduction

Welcome to Green305 ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about how we collect, use, and safeguard your information. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and use when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Location Data

We access your device's location services to provide core app functionality, including:

  • Displaying nearby green spaces and calculating distances
  • Enabling location-based check-ins at verified locations
  • Providing navigation assistance

Location data is processed in real-time on your device and is not transmitted to or stored on external servers.

Locally Stored Data

The following information is stored locally on your device:

  • Visit history and check-in records
  • User preferences and settings
  • Display name (if provided)
  • Achievement and progress data

Third-Party Services

We use Firebase Firestore to retrieve location data for green spaces displayed in the app. No personal information is transmitted to Firebase.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above to:

  • Display nearby green spaces and eco-friendly locations
  • Calculate distances and provide navigation
  • Enable and verify location-based check-ins
  • Track your exploration progress and achievements locally
  • Personalize your experience within the app

4. Data Storage and Security

All personal data, including visit history, preferences, and achievements, is stored exclusively on your device using iOS secure storage mechanisms. We do not maintain external servers that store your personal information.

Your data remains under your control and can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the application or clearing app data through your device settings.

5. Third-Party Services

Green305 integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Firestore: Used to retrieve green space location data. No personal information is transmitted.
  • Apple Maps: Used for navigation and directions functionality.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your locally stored data at any time within the app
  • Delete all your data by uninstalling the application
  • Disable location services through your device settings
  • Control notification preferences within the app

7. Children's Privacy

Green305 is designed for users of all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app encourages outdoor exploration and environmental awareness in a family-friendly manner.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
